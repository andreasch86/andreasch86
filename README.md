## 🧠 Who Am I?

I'm a **Senior ML Engineer** based in Cape Town with a background that's a little unusual: I started in **Applied Mathematics and Mathematical Biology** — which means I actually understand *why* the models work, not just *how to call the API*.

I've been building ML systems since **2016** — back when "MLOps" wasn't even a word yet. From university lecturing and research to productionising ML for major financial institutions, I've done the full journey.

> **What sets me apart in the age of AI tools?**
> I know the maths. I understand the theory. I care about getting it *right* in production — not just getting it to run.
